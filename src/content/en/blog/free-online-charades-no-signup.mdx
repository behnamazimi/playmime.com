## Why no‑signup matters

The longer your group spends creating accounts and downloading apps, the colder the room gets. A browser‑based game with instant entry keeps energy up and lowers the barrier for casual players and kids.

---

## Start in three taps

1. Visit [/play](/en/play).
2. Choose a mode (Quick Play for speed, One Word Per Turn for strategy, Heads‑Up for chaos).
3. Share your screen or rotate the device for in‑person play.

That’s it. You’re in the game.

---

## House rules for smooth sessions

- No speaking or spelling the secret word.
- Use shared gestures: number of words, “sounds like,” book/movie/song icons.
- 60–90 seconds per turn, 1 point per correct guess.
- One pass allowed if a prompt stalls.

These lightweight rules work for any group without long explanations.

---

## Great prompts without prep

Use built‑in word banks by category — Animals, Actions, Movies, Sports, Office Life, and more. If you have a theme night, pick the closest category and let the game do the rest.

---

## Keep the party moving

- Rotate actors quickly; momentum is more important than perfect scores.
- Play upbeat music between rounds.
- Celebrate dramatic fails and last‑second wins — the best stories come from both.

Free online charades removes the friction so you can focus on what matters: connection, creativity, and a lot of laughter. Try a round now and see how fast the room lights up.
