## Game Modes Explained

### Quick Play
Race against the clock to guess as many words as possible. Perfect for competitive groups who want fast-paced action.

### One Word Per Turn
Take turns guessing one word at a time. This mode emphasizes teamwork and strategy, making it great for groups who want a more collaborative experience.

### Heads-up
Hold your device to your forehead while teammates give clues. This classic charades format works perfectly for in-person game nights.

### Random Word Generator
No rules, just words. Perfect for practicing your acting skills or casual play without the pressure of competition.
