## Tips for Playing with Teens

To make charades successful with teenagers:

1. **Use current references**: Stay up to date with pop culture
2. **Keep it relevant**: Choose words that match their interests
3. **Respect their space**: Don't force participation if they're not interested
4. **Make it competitive**: Teens often enjoy competitive elements
5. **Allow creativity**: Let them interpret words in their own way
6. **Keep it fun**: Don't make it feel like a school activity

## Age-Appropriate Content

When choosing words for teens:

- Avoid overly childish words
- Include current trends and references
- Match their maturity level
- Consider their interests and hobbies
- Keep it relatable to their experiences
