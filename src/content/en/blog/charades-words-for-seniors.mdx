- Creates joy and laughter

## Tips for Playing with Seniors

To make charades enjoyable for seniors:

1. **Use familiar references**: Choose words from their era and experiences
2. **Keep it simple**: Avoid overly complex or abstract concepts
3. **Be patient**: Allow time for thinking and acting
4. **Encourage participation**: Make everyone feel welcome and valued
5. **Celebrate effort**: Appreciate all attempts, not just correct guesses
6. **Create comfort**: Ensure a relaxed, supportive atmosphere

## Physical Considerations

When playing with seniors:

- Allow seated participation if needed
- Keep rounds at a comfortable length
- Provide clear, visible demonstrations
- Use larger gestures that are easier to see
- Be mindful of hearing and vision limitations
