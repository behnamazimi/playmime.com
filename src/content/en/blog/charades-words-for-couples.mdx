## Tips for Couple Charades

To make charades fun for couples:

1. **Keep it light**: Focus on fun, not competition
2. **Use inside jokes**: Incorporate your shared experiences
3. **Be supportive**: Encourage each other's attempts
4. **Make it romantic**: Add candles, music, or special treats
5. **Create memories**: Take photos of funny moments
6. **Enjoy the process**: The goal is connection, not perfection
