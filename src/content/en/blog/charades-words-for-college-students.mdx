## Tips for College Charades

To make charades successful with students:

1. **Keep it relevant**: Use words that match their experiences
2. **Make it social**: Focus on group bonding
3. **Keep it affordable**: No expensive equipment needed
4. **Allow creativity**: Let students interpret words freely
5. **Make it fun**: The goal is stress relief and connection
6. **Be flexible**: Adapt to different group sizes and spaces
